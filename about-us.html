Our Distinguished Legacy

Welcome to Shri Gurunanak Mission H.S.School (Hindi/English Medium), Premnagar, Jabalpur. Established in 1959, our institution was founded to serve the growing educational needs of students in our community.

श्री गुरुनानक मिशन उ.मा.शाला, प्रेमनगर, जबलपुर सन 1959 में समाज के हर स्तर के विद्यार्थियों को शिक्षा उपलब्ध कराने के लिए प्रारम्भ किया गया है।

We are an institution where students belonging to all castes, religions, and creeds are motivated to become good scholars. Following the teachings of Guru Gobind Singh Ji's words "शुभ करमन ते कबहुँ न टरों" (Never deviate from righteous deeds), our school continuously strives to embody these noble principles in our educational approach.

For over six decades, we have been dedicated to providing quality education while nurturing values of integrity, inclusivity, and academic excellence. Our mission remains steadfast in creating an environment where every student can flourish regardless of their background.

Our World-Class Facilities

Environments that inspire learning and growth

Library Interior

Knowledge Resource Center

Our 10,000+ volume library with digital archives, quiet study zones, and collaborative spaces fosters a love for reading and research.

Science Laboratory

Advanced Science Labs

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories with modern instrumentation for hands-on scientific exploration.

Sports Complex

Sports Complex

Olympic-sized swimming pool, indoor stadium, athletic track, and specialized courts for basketball, tennis, and badminton.

Performing Arts Center

Creative Arts Center

Dedicated spaces for music, dance, drama, and visual arts with professional studios and exhibition galleries.

Technology Center

Innovation & Technology Hub

Cutting-edge computer labs, robotics workshop, 3D printing facility, and maker space for STEM innovation.

Wellness Center

Health & Wellness Center
