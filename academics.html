Excellence in Academic Pursuits

Our CBSE-affiliated curriculum is designed to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and character development. We provide a holistic educational experience that prepares students for global citizenship.

CBSE Curriculum Framework

A comprehensive educational approach aligned with national standards

Curriculum Overview

Our academic program follows the CBSE curriculum, enhanced with value-added components to develop well-rounded individuals:

  • Nursery to Grade XII education following CBSE guidelines
  • Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) system
  • Emphasis on holistic development through co-scholastic activities
  • Integration of technology in teaching and learning processes
  • Special focus on environmental education and sustainability
  • Value education integrated across all subjects

Curriculum Highlights

Our enhanced CBSE curriculum includes distinctive features that set us apart:

  • STEM-focused learning with dedicated innovation labs
  • Language acquisition programs in French and Sanskrit
  • Financial literacy and entrepreneurship modules
  • Digital citizenship and cyber safety curriculum
  • Project-based learning across all grade levels
  • Career counseling from Grade IX onwards

Assessment System

Comprehensive evaluation for holistic development

Continuous Assessment

Our assessment framework focuses on the all-round development of students through continuous evaluation:

1
Formative Assessment

Regular class tests, quizzes, projects, and presentations to monitor ongoing progress.

2
Summative Assessment

Term-end examinations to evaluate comprehensive understanding of concepts.

3
Practical Evaluation

Laboratory work, field studies, and hands-on application of concepts.

Holistic Evaluation

Beyond academics, we assess students' development across multiple dimensions:

4
Co-Scholastic Areas

Assessment of life skills, attitudes, values, and participation in co-curricular activities.

5
Portfolio Assessment

Collection of student work demonstrating effort, progress, and achievements.

6
Self & Peer Assessment

Developing metacognitive skills through reflection and evaluation of own work.
