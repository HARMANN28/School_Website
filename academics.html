Excellence in Academic Pursuits

Our MP Board affiliated curriculum is designed to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and character development. We provide a holistic educational experience that prepares students for global citizenship.

MP Board Curriculum Framework

A comprehensive educational approach aligned with state standards

Curriculum Overview

Our academic program follows the MP Board curriculum, enhanced with value-added components to develop well-rounded individuals:

  • Classes I to XII education following MP Board guidelines
  • Hindi and English medium instruction available
  • Emphasis on holistic development through co-curricular activities
  • Integration of moral values and ethical education
  • Special focus on character building and discipline
  • Value education integrated across all subjects as per Sikh principles

Curriculum Highlights

Our enhanced MP Board curriculum includes distinctive features that set us apart:

  • Bilingual education in Hindi and English medium
  • Strong foundation in Mathematics and Science
  • Comprehensive language development programs
  • Cultural and traditional knowledge integration
  • Regular assessment and evaluation system
  • Career guidance and counseling from higher classes

Teaching Methodology

Innovative approaches that foster deep learning and critical thinking

Collaborative Learning

Students work in groups to solve problems, complete tasks, and create projects, developing teamwork and communication skills.

Inquiry-Based Learning

Students develop questions, investigate solutions, and build new understandings through research and discovery.

Technology Integration

Smart classrooms, learning management systems, and digital resources enhance engagement and learning outcomes.

Project-Based Learning

Extended projects that allow students to investigate and respond to complex questions or challenges.

Differentiated Instruction

Teaching tailored to accommodate diverse learning styles, abilities, and interests within the classroom.

Global Perspective

Curriculum connects local issues to global contexts, developing international-mindedness in students.

Assessment System

Comprehensive evaluation for holistic development

Continuous Assessment

Our assessment framework focuses on the all-round development of students through continuous evaluation:

1
Formative Assessment

Regular class tests, quizzes, projects, and presentations to monitor ongoing progress.

2
Summative Assessment

Term-end examinations to evaluate comprehensive understanding of concepts.

3
Practical Evaluation

Laboratory work, field studies, and hands-on application of concepts.

Holistic Evaluation

Beyond academics, we assess students' development across multiple dimensions:

4
Co-Scholastic Areas

Assessment of life skills, attitudes, values, and participation in co-curricular activities.

5
Portfolio Assessment

Collection of student work demonstrating effort, progress, and achievements.

6
Self & Peer Assessment

Developing metacognitive skills through reflection and evaluation of own work.
